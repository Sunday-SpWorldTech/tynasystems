Our Story

From WhatsApp chaos to founder freedom.

I started Tyna Systems LLC because I watched too many brilliant Founders burn out running ₦20M/year businesses from WhatsApp.

Before this, I was building operations for fast-growing companies, but I kept seeing the same chaos back home: no systems, no SOPs, no exit.

Now I build operating systems in Notion that allow founders to take vacations without revenue dropping. Because Businesses deserve to scale without the founder breaking.
