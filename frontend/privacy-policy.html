Use and Sharing

How we use and share your data

  • To deliver services: Set up your Notion and ClickUp systems, run automations and provide support.
  • To communicate: Send proposals, invoices and onboarding links through email or WhatsApp.
  • To improve: Review anonymous analytics on site usage.

We may share data with approved service providers including Notion, ClickUp, Stripe, Wise, Google Workspace, Calendly, Make.com and Zapier where needed to deliver services. We never sell your data and we sign DPAs with sub-processors where required.

Platform downloads priced at $6.99 are independently created by our developers for learning, testing and platform previewnstration purposes. They do not contain, use or derive from client data.

International data transfers

Your data may be stored in the US or EU through tools such as Notion, ClickUp and Google. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and NDPA adequacy protections.

Retention

Client data is kept for the contract duration plus 3 years for legal or tax purposes. Inquiry data is kept for 12 months if you do not become a client. Workspace data belongs to you and we remove our access within 30 days after transfer.

Your Rights

Your rights under NDPA and GDPR

  • Request access to data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to marketing communications.
  • Export your data to CSV or JSON where available.

Email privacy@tynasystems.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

Data is protected by encryption in transit and at rest through Notion and ClickUp. Access is limited to your assigned Tyna team and you.

Cookies

We use essential and analytics cookies only. Users can accept or decline analytics cookies on first visit. We do not use ad trackers.

Children

Tyna Systems does not target or knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.
